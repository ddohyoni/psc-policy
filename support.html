FAQ

Q: Score announcements are not playing.

A: Please check your device volume and make sure silent mode is off. You can also try changing the TTS voice in Settings.

Q: How do I start a game with rally scoring?

A: When creating a new match, select "Rally" as the scoring type in the game settings.

Q: Can I use the app on Apple Watch?

A: Yes! The Apple Watch companion app lets you control scoring directly from your wrist. Make sure the Watch app is installed and your iPhone is nearby.

Q: How do I restore my purchase?

A: Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases". Your purchase is linked to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device.
