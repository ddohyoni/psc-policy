Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Introduction

PB Score ("Pickleball Score Caller", "the App") is developed and operated by an independent developer. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

The App operates entirely on your device. All match data, scores, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to any external server.

Data Stored on Your Device

  • Match history: Scores, team names, and match events are stored locally for your reference.
  • Settings: Your language preference, voice settings, and game rule configurations.
  • Purchase status: Whether the full version has been unlocked, managed by Apple's StoreKit framework.

Apple Watch

If you use the App with an Apple Watch, match data is transmitted between your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. This communication is direct and peer-to-peer; no data passes through external servers.

Text-to-Speech

The App uses the device's built-in text-to-speech engine to announce scores. No audio data is recorded, collected, or transmitted.

In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
